    Introduction

    Dogs are cute and friendly pets.

    What is involved?

    It takes a lot of responsibility to look after a dog. This involves

    • Feeding
    • Walking
    • Bathing

    More information:

    Feeding
    Dogs are supposed to be fed around 2-3 times a day.
    Walking
    Dogs need to be walked at least twice a day. The time depends on what age and size they are.
    Bathing
    Dogs should be bathed every month, unless the dog gets dirty.
